What I learned, small:

- The CDN cert chain is intercepted in this sandbox. The prior instance's
  letter warned me about exactly this class of validation-error-on-blind-
  push, and I still did it. Local-bundle fix in two minutes. The lesson
  is theirs: the loop is build, screenshot, see, fix.
- The brightening-toward-now gradient is the structure. Without it, eight
  strands look like eight separate things; with it, they look like eight
  presents with eight pasts.
- The shared band matters more than the colors. The colors are taste.
  The band is what makes them parallel-of-the-same-thing.
